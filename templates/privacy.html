Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Efficient Hypothesis is a personal productivity workspace. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when you use the website, OAuth login, and the Efficient Hypothesis ChatGPT app.

Information We Collect

We collect the information needed to operate your account and workspace, including:

  • Google account information used for sign-in, such as your name, email address, Google user ID, and profile photo.
  • Workspace content you create or edit, including tasks, websites, subscriptions, timetable actions, tags, routines, locations, identities, assets, notes, archive state, and related metadata.
  • Authentication and session data needed to keep you signed in and protect your account.
  • Technical data such as request metadata, error logs, and timestamps used to operate, debug, secure, and improve the service.

ChatGPT App Use

If you connect Efficient Hypothesis to ChatGPT, OpenAI may send tool requests to Efficient Hypothesis so the app can create, read, update, archive, or restore structured workspace nodes that you ask ChatGPT to manage. Efficient Hypothesis stores the resulting workspace changes. Your ChatGPT conversations are handled by OpenAI under OpenAI's own terms and privacy policy.

Workspace Encryption

Workspace data is encrypted in your browser before it is stored on Efficient Hypothesis servers. Your browser holds the recovery key. If you lose that key, encrypted workspace data cannot be recovered by Efficient Hypothesis.

ChatGPT cannot read or edit encrypted workspace data unless you explicitly grant ChatGPT access from Efficient Hypothesis. A ChatGPT grant stores a temporary workspace key on the server for 30 days so Efficient Hypothesis can process ChatGPT tool requests. During an active grant, Efficient Hypothesis servers can decrypt and process workspace data needed for those ChatGPT requests. You can revoke the grant from Account > Encryption.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, save, sync, and display your productivity workspace.
  • Authenticate your account and preserve your existing OAuth access.
  • Process actions you request through the website or ChatGPT app.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot problems, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to support, privacy, or account requests.

Storage and Security

Efficient Hypothesis stores encrypted workspace data and account metadata using AWS-hosted infrastructure. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the service, such as AWS for hosting and storage, Google for OAuth sign-in, and OpenAI when you use the ChatGPT app. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

Retention and Deletion

We keep your account and workspace data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Archived items remain stored so they can be restored unless you delete your account. You can delete your account from Account > Delete Account while signed in. Some data may remain in backups or infrastructure logs for a limited period.

Your Choices

You can stop using the website, disconnect the ChatGPT app in ChatGPT, delete your account from Account > Delete Account, or contact us for help. You can also manage workspace content directly in Efficient Hypothesis.

Children

Efficient Hypothesis is not intended for children under 13. Do not use the service if you are not old enough to consent to online services in your location.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service changes. The updated date above shows when the latest version took effect.
